Asset

The Park Bearer Scrip Token
Bearer scrip is a unique opportunity to own part of a crypto asset. Each Bearer scrip is the value of 1/2000th of a car parking space at a UK airport. This process is known as digitalisation, whereby the title asset remains but the value of the asset is realised in the creation of each Bearer scrip. This is very similar to the issuance of shares in a company. PARK Bearer scrip is represented by a digital token, which once purchased can be sold or traded via specialist crypto currency exchanges or the Omni decentralised exchange (online peer to peer market).
PARK
PARK makes it possible for people around the world to own an interest in airport parking spaces, that pay a regular fixed income to scrip holders. PARK can be easily sent and traded with anyone in the world. PARK enables liquidity for property holders and individuals using the secondary market, to take advantage of the stable yield offered by the property (scrip).

The title to commercial real estate assets - parking lots in major UK airports - as well as a set of cashflow contracts, are legally owned by Limited Liability Companies (LLCs). PARK is issued by a management corporation that manages the LLCs. PARK is bearer scrip, but can be redeemed for common stock in the management corporation.
Cashflow distributions paid to common stock holders, as to the holders of PARK tokens, may be subject to less optimal taxation and more filing overhead than being the holder of membership in one of the LLCs and receiving the cashflow directly as pass-through-income. That means, you are responsible for paying tax on the income in your home jurisdiction, or if a US person, as ordinary income at your marginal tax rate - with no tax overheads, no filings concerns, no reporting needed other than to add the income to your annual tax statement.
LLC tokens are issued to replace PARK tokens redeemed, these are indivisible tokens that must not be moved from the address requested, or a change of Bitcoin address must be reported to update our register, in order to ensure there is an identity match between the registered owner and the controller of the address where the LLC tokens are held. Then, a larger cashflow distribution payment, which does not involve any tax withholding as affects the PARK token, will hit addresses containing LLC tokens.
The added benefit of LLC membership is security - your registered identity is kept in the management company’s records and is protected by the privacy provisions Wyoming courts. If the LLC tokens are lost due to theft of a private key controlling a Bitcoin address, or perhaps a water mishap that fried the hard drive, a new tranche of tokens can easily be issued to replace the old ones, with that particular LLC’s cashflow distributions routing to them.
The PARK product is structured like a trust but using the provisions of LLC organization and they By Laws of the management corporation. The management corporation has deferred ownership of its common stock to act as a pool-in-common for the purpose of functioning as a Real Estate Investment Trust, and no officer holds a beneficial interest in the common stock. The holding LLC operating agreements are extremely rigid and define only a few discrete actions that can be taken with the assets, and a strict pro-rata distribution of cashflow received minus a 2% fee. There are provisions for placing holders of PARK into LLC membership and visa versa, provisions for acquiring a specific number of properties, and no flexibility for management to liquidate assets except by election of members to exercise a buy-back option after a five year term. The management team is subject to regulatory oversight in terms of disclosed information associated with the security offering by the SEC in the United States and the FCA in the United Kingdom, as well as accounting standards to meet in conjunction with the requirements of the IRS and HMRC. This product rests on the shoulders of some of the strongest legal systems in the world with the most efficacious regulatory environments for financial products.

Tether Euros (EURT) are also in circulation and redeemable for SEPA deposits on OpenLedger.

Scrip is an old word going back to the early days of banking, when gold smiths would issue gold scrip redeemable for hard bullion. Scrip in the modern day means a provisional certificate of money issued by bank or company, entitling the holder to redeem it for formal stock registration and dividends. Based on the Wyoming Business Corporation Act, C-corporations can issue a bearer scrip in whole or fractional form, the blockchain token that PARK holders control is such a scrip.
In finance, a derivative is a contract that derives its value from the performance of an underlying asset. The PARK bearer scrip is an equity derivative for the common stock of Blockchain Parking Management Unlevered. The management company allows scrip holders to deliver PARK to a company-controlled address and request registration in LLC membership, constituting a direct equity ownership, which allows higher yield and recourse for private key loss.

Much of this mining power is concentrated in the top 5 mining pools, but also the composition of that top 5 has been very fluid compared to other industries.
